Nicola Ken Barozzi

(nicolaken at apache dot org)

Nicola Ken Barozzi is a committer for Jakarta POI, which he helped in entering Jakarta, Avalon, and for Apache Cocoon and Apache Forrest on Xml-Apache.

The work with these projects have made him found the Krysalis Community Project, with the aim of incubating his own and other projects till/if a community creates round them.

He started the Krysalis Centipede build system to make building projects a simple thing, since it had become a too common activity to be a pleasure.

FAQ: You can call him Nicola, Ken, or Nicola Ken, as you wish.
