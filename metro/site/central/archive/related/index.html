Apache Projects Overview of a number of Apache projects that leverage the Avalon framework, containers, and shared components. The page includes details of the James Enterprise Messaging Platform that leverages the Avalon component model and several of the Cornerstone common components; the Turbine Fulcrum component suite and development related to the Merlin container, and the Cocoon project that is leveraging the Fortress container as part of their overall development strategy.

This list includes a number of container iniatives that leverage the Avalon container contract together with third-party container extensions, through to dedication applications such the OpenIM instant messaging project.
