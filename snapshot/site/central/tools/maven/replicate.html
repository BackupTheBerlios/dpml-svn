Merlin Plugin for Maven
Utilities

The Merlin Plugin for Maven includes a utility goal supporting the replication of jar artifacts form the Maven repository to the Merlin repository.

Goals
GoalDescription
merlin:replicate

Creates a duplicate of the project jar artifact contained in the Maven repository and includes it under the Merlin repository.
merlin:install

Is equivalent to jar:install followed by merlin:replicate.
